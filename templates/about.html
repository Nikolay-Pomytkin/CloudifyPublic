About

Based on Receiptify, Cloudify generates a list of a Spotify users top 50 artists in a unique wordcloud. Written entirely in Python, your wordcloud is generated from the WordCloud library by Andreas Mueller and the font used in the wordcloud is KeepOnTruckin by the BrainEaters.

Check out these awesome people and projects if you have a chance!

Note: Unfortunately with the font, Chinese and Arabic characters are not supported :(
